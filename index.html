Davide Scognamiglio

I'm Davide, i'm 24 and I am a photographer
and videomaker based in Naples, Italy

One thing I've always loved about photography is catching time.
Whatever you're living right now is temporary.
A special event, a beautiful trip, a walk with your friends:
these things will all be gone in the blink of an eye.

My mission is to make your memories live forever through photography,
so you can focus on the moments you're living.
Not thinking about before, nor later.
Let's get together into the while.
